A Precision Workflow for Every Learner

Granular Insights over Final Marks

When your tracking sheet includes marks for specific questions (e.g. Q1: Fractions), AI can spot the story behind the grade. This allows for precisely targeted support that a single percentage would hide.

NotebookLM

1. Grounding & Analysis

Upload your tracking sheet. This ensures the AI's "brain" is limited only to your students' actual evidence.

How AI helps: It reads across your whole sheet to find links—instantly identifying which skills are holding students back from higher overall marks.
NotebookLM

2. Diagnosing Misconceptions

"Based on the results, identify which specific topics have the widest gap in class performance. Which students scored highly overall but struggled with one specific sub-topic?"
How AI helps: It flags "invisible" gaps—identifying students who look fine on paper but have specific, hidden misconceptions.
The Hand-off: Take the findings from NotebookLM and pop them into Gemini to plan your support tasks.
Gemini

3. Strategic Grouping

"Review these gaps. Group the class into three tiers based on mastery of [Topic], ensuring those with specific misconceptions are clustered together."
How AI helps: AI assists with logistical clustering, grouping students by the nature of their error rather than just ability.
Gemini

4. Resource Synthesis

Generate targeted scaffolds, word banks, or challenge tasks for each identified group.

Trick: Ask Gemini to "Create a 10-minute reteach activity for the group that struggled with [Specific Topic]."

Classroom Example

Discovery: High-attainers failed the Decimals section while acing Algebra.
Intervention: Gemini suggests a visual scaffold for the Decimals group while others do extensions.

The AI Prompt Bank

Copy and paste these templates to get started.

NotebookLM (Analysis)

The "Gap Finder"

Best for finding hidden trends in your tracking sheet.

"Analyze the student data. Identify which specific question or sub-topic has the lowest average score. List the students who struggled with this topic despite having high scores in other areas."

The "Growth Narrative"

Best for parent meetings or report comments.

"Looking at the prior knowledge column vs the total score, identify the top 3 'most improved' students and explain exactly which topics they mastered to make that jump."

Gemini (Action)

The "Three-Tier Task"

Paste the analysis from NotebookLM before this prompt.

"Based on these groups, create a single lesson task on [Topic]. 
Tier 1: Scaffolded with a word bank. 
Tier 2: Standard practice. 
Tier 3: Abstract challenge with no prompts."

The "Reteach Script"

Generate a quick intervention plan.

"Write a 5-minute explanation script for a small group intervention focusing on the misconception that [Specific Error]. Use a simple analogy suitable for a 10-year-old."
