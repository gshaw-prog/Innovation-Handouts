Data to Differentiation

A Precision Workflow for Every Learner

Granular Insights over Final Marks

When your tracking sheet includes marks for specific questions (e.g. Q1: Fractions), AI can spot the story behind the grade. This allows for precisely targeted support that a single percentage would hide.

NotebookLM

1. Grounding & Analysis

Upload your tracking sheet (CSV or Google Sheet). This ensures the AI's "brain" is limited only to your students' actual evidence.

How AI helps: It reads across your whole sheet to find links—instantly identifying which skills are holding students back from higher overall marks.
"Identify the top 3 concepts mastered and the top 2 where a significant performance gap exists across the class. Which students show a high pre-test score but struggled with the final assessment?"
NotebookLM

2. Diagnosing Misconceptions

Ask the AI to look past the totals and into the question-level data to find the 'Why'.

How AI helps: It flags "invisible" gaps—identifying students who look fine on paper but have specific, hidden misconceptions in foundational topics.
"Based on the results, identify which specific topics have the widest gap in class performance. Which students scored highly overall but struggled with one specific sub-topic (e.g. Mixed Fractions)?"

The Hand-off Phase

Copy the identified gaps and student groups from NotebookLM and paste them into Gemini to generate your actual classroom resources.

Gemini

3. Strategic Grouping

Use Gemini to turn the data analysis into a seating plan or intervention schedule.

How AI helps: It automates the heavy lifting of sorting 30+ students into meaningful clusters based on their exact needs rather than just 'High/Middle/Low'.
"Review these gaps. Group the class into three tiers based on mastery of [Topic], ensuring those with specific misconceptions are clustered together for a focused reteach session."
Gemini

4. Resource Synthesis

Generate targeted scaffolds, word banks, or challenge tasks for each identified group.

How AI helps: It can instantly write three different versions of the same task, providing the 'just-right' level of support for every desk in the room.
Expert Tip: Ask Gemini to "Create a 10-minute reteach activity for the group that struggled with [Specific Topic], including a visual analogy and 3 check-for-understanding questions."
