Data to Differentiation

A Precision Workflow for Every Learner

Granular Insights over Final Marks

When your tracking sheet includes marks for specific questions or topics (e.g. Q1: Fractions, Q4: Decimals), AI can spot the story behind the grade. Seeing that a student scored 70% overall but got 0/5 on Decimal Addition allows for precisely targeted support that a single percentage would hide.

Workflow Steps

NotebookLM

1. Grounding & Analysis

Upload your tracking sheet. This ensures the AI's "brain" is limited only to your students' actual evidence.

How AI helps: It acts as a "private expert." It reads across your whole sheet to find links between topics—instantly identifying which skills are holding students back from higher overall marks.
NotebookLM

2. Diagnosing Misconceptions

"Based on the results, identify which specific topics have the widest gap in class performance. Which students scored highly overall but struggled with one specific sub-topic?"
How AI helps: It uses pattern recognition to flag "invisible" gaps—identifying students who have specific, hidden misconceptions that will block future progress.
Next Step: Take the findings from NotebookLM and pop them into Gemini to plan your support tasks.
Gemini

3. Strategic Grouping

"Review these topic gaps. Group the class into three tiers based on their mastery of [Topic Name], ensuring that those with specific misconceptions are clustered together."
How AI helps: AI assists with logistical clustering, grouping students by the specific nature of their error (e.g. the "Fractions" group) rather than just general ability.
Gemini

4. Resource Synthesis

Generate targeted scaffolds, word banks, or challenge tasks for each identified group.

How AI helps: Through creative synthesis, Gemini can instantly create multiple versions of the same task tailored to the specific needs of each group identified in your tracking sheet.
Trick: Ask Gemini to "Create a 10-minute reteach activity for the group that struggled with [Specific Topic]."

Classroom Example

Discovery: The tracking data shows that while most aced Algebra, a small group of high-attainers failed the Decimals section.
Intervention: Gemini suggests a visual scaffold for the Decimals group while the rest move to an extension task.
